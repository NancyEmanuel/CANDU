How to Navigate in the Virtual Reality Game

This article will teach users how to navigate in the Virtual Reality Environment by utilizing their VR headsets, or keyboard and mouse. When users enter the scene they will automatically be given the VR prefab controls assuming they do not have a headset. To move around users must utilize the awsd keys instead of the up,down,left, and right arrows. Press W to move up, S to move backwards, A to move to the left, and D to move to the right.
